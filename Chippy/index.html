About the game

Chippy's GameBox collects quick mini-games like Catch, Dash, and Ping-Pong into short, replayable sessions. Each session randomly selects three mini-games for varied, competitive fun.

Features

  • Randomized mini-games every session
  • Smart AI with balanced challenge
  • Single-player & Dual-player local play
  • Retro 8-bit pixel art and chiptune sound
Platform: Android
Modes: Single-player & Dual-player
Connectivity: Offline
